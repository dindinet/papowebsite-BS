Optimised Websites For Business Growth

Solo Entrepreneurs and Small Businesses

Get more from your website budget

Search Ready

Send the right signals to the search engines to get found by your target audience.

Like all businesses engaged in digital marketing you will want your website to feature in search engine results for your service or product. Your success at achieving this will depend on a number of factors, the most important being your content  and how well it aligns with your potential visitors and their needs. All your great content can fail if the pages that present it are poorly coded. Your content needs help to get found. A platform that focuses on building your pages to give them the best chance of being found is essential.

Fast Load Speed

Keep visitors focused on your offer and influence google search positioning.

There is nothing more off putting to web users than a slow loading site. If you allow your potential customers attention to wander they will click away into the waiting arms of your competition. Worse still, Google marks down slow loading sites in its search ranking. We aim for a page load time of 2 seconds or less. Design decisions have a huge impact on load times. Sometimes Less really is more.

Responsive

Engage and convert visitors on all screen sizes. Mobile, Desktop and everyting in-between.

A responsive design is one where your content displays appropriately on all screen sizes. With more and more people choosing mobile devices as their main method to access the internet google will reward sites that offer an optimum experience for mobile visitors. There is more to a responsive site than just displaying well on different sized screens. When making design decisions about what is absolutely necessary for your minimum viable website you should focus on the largest user group which according to google is a small screen mobile user with a slow connection speed.  Once you get data from your live site and real visitors you can make better decisions.

Manage Your Content

Easy peasy publising at Internet Speed with a choice of simple to use Content Management Systems (CMS).

A CMS allows you and your team to maintain existing content and add new content to your website quickly and easily. The CMS adapts as new content types are added. All content is stored in human readable files. [The CMS does not rely on complex and expensive to maintain database systems.]

Build and Deploy

Publish your latest content changes in real time or in batch mode. You are in charge.

Your website platform will consist of 4 main swappable components which work together. A Git repository( file storage)A CMS (file editing tool)A build and deploy procedure (to publish the latest version of your website)Live hosting. [explicancio]A repository (where all your content lives), a CMS (to edit and add new content to the repository), a build and deploy service (which takes your content and creates all the pages and uploads them to the hosting) and, finally, a hosting service. All of these components can be customised to suite your requirements. As better components in each category become available swapping the old ones out for new ones is possible.

Multi Location Hosting

Your website duplicated to be closer to your audience and always served over a secure connection. Faster and safer.

Hosted with a secure certificate(https) on a multi location Content Delivery Network your website will withstand all that the internet can throw at it 24/7. No more worrying about server security or optimisation. Apart from no maintenance to worry about the main advantages of a CDN are speed of delivery and ultra availability. Your visitors will always receive your content served by the server nearest to their physical location. Using a CDN means that your website will never grind to a halt under load. [High traffic. Cyber attacks. Network outages.]

Auto-Backups

A historical record of every change made to content or site design is maintained. Think undo/redo for your whole website.

All your content and the supporting files that combine to make your completed website are kept safely in a Git repository. Git is a genious system for storing your files. You may never understand it fully but rest assured that having Git take care of your files means you can sleep soundly. One massive advantage is automatic continuous backups of your whole project. Learn moreGit tracks all changes to files and keeps a historical record of them. You can revert back to any point in the history. Permission is given to the CMS and the build service to access your Git repository. Furthermore you or anyone with permission can make a copy of your repo, including its history, to work on. The new work can then be merged back into the main repository. This is useful when developers need to make multiple changes to the website layout and see the results without affecting the live website. Git is a godsend to any project.Git is a version control system (VCS) for tracking changes in computer files and coordinating work on those files among multiple people or systems.A repository is a folder or group of folders and their files that are under the control of Git.
